# Grocery-Profitability-Overview-Dashboard

## Business Context
This project analyses company sales and profitability data to uncover key profit drivers, identify underperforming areas, and provide actionable business recommendations. The goal is to support **data-driven strategic decision-making** across sales channels, product categories, and geographic regions.


![1770316393149](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1b3b1e15-4063-438b-bf66-d49a02a6807d)

## Business Problem
The company lacks visibility into its profitability performance across **sales channels, products, sub-categories, categories, and regions**, making it difficult to identify where profit is truly being generated and where strategic focus should be applied. This limits effective decision-making related to **investment prioritisation, pricing strategy, inventory planning, and regional expansion**.

---
## Analytical Approach
To address this challenge, I conducted a structured analytical process involving:

- Data cleaning and transformation  
- Feature engineering to compute key KPIs (Revenue, Profit, Margin, Discount Impact)  
- Multi-dimensional analysis across:
  - Sales channels  
  - Product categories and sub-categories  
  - Individual products  
  - Discount levels  
  - Geographic regions  
- Development of interactive dashboards to visualise performance drivers and profitability patterns  

---
## Key Insights

- **High overall profitability:**  
  The business generated **$31.6M profit from $55.4M revenue**, demonstrating strong financial performance and stable results throughout the year.

- **Channel contribution:**  
  - **Store channel:** 57% of total profit (primary profit driver)  
  - **Online channel:** 21% of total profit (strong growth potential)  
  - **Catalog & Reseller:** Lowest contribution, underperforming  

- **Product performance:**  
  - Profit is concentrated in **Computers and Cameras**  
  - **Projector products**, particularly **Proseware and Contoso**, deliver the highest returns  

- **Pricing impact:**  
  **No-discount transactions generate the highest profit**, indicating strong customer willingness to pay full price for high-value products.

- **Regional trends:**  
  **North America leads profitability**, while other regions significantly underperform, highlighting international growth opportunities.

---

## Business Impact
This analysis provides leadership with actionable insights that:

- Clearly identify **profit drivers and underperforming areas**  
- Support **data-driven channel investment decisions**  
- Improve **pricing strategy and margin optimisation**  
- Enable **strategic geographic expansion planning**  

These insights empower leadership to **maximise profitability, optimise operations, and scale revenue growth**.

---
##  Recommendations

- **Channel Strategy:**  
  Prioritise investment and optimisation efforts in **Store and Online channels**, while reassessing the strategic value of **Catalog and Reseller channels**.

- **Product Focus:**  
  Allocate inventory, marketing, and supplier investments toward **high-margin categories and top-performing projector products**.

- **Pricing Optimisation:**  
  Maintain a **value-based pricing strategy** and restrict discounting to underperforming or slow-moving products.

- **Geographic Expansion:**  
  Replicate **North America’s sales and operational strategies** in lower-performing regions to unlock global growth.
